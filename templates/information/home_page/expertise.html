Expertise

CBS acts as a reliable partner at every stage of the project: from "concept to reality"

Roofings


In 2003, Indian building industry was introduced to modular polycarbonate panels manufactured by DANPAL (Israel) and soon CBS was incorporated as a solution provider for carrying out the base structures and coverings with polycarbonate, which was extensively used as a element of daylight architecture. CBS specializes in executing aesthetically designed large span skylights, and established itself as a favorite of many leading architecture studios and big reality developers in the country.

Steel Structures


Having 14 years of experience in providing solutions for covering atriums at some of the major retail facilities in Delhi-NCR and many other cities across the country. CBS has mastered the skills of executing sleek, elegant, economical and sturdy steel structures for claddings, polycarbonate panels, membrane, mesh and other building requirements. CBS is equipped with an in-house structure design and drawing team to compliment the smooth execution of the project undertaken.

Tensile Membrane Structures


To cater the demand of the industry, new products and materials provided flexibility and advantage in opting for irregular shapes and forms to the roofings and coverings. This advantage provided was provided by architectural fabric which came into vogue and soon became a favorite of architects and designers. CBS looked for the future in membrane structures half a decade ago and successfully executed a number of atriums, entrances of housing and township projects.

Facades and Claddings


Acceptance of innovative and environment friendly building materials like membrane, glass, mesh, composite panel, to name a few give a unique look and dimension to the front/facias of the buildings. The specialized facade designing studios and agencies are supported by CBS in realising the concept with effective and clean execution.
