About Us

"Concept to Reality"



Connect Building Solutions Pvt. Ltd. undertakes projects from conceptualization to commissioning. CBS designs, and executes a range of architectural elements from steel structures, tensile membrane structures, space frames to facades, roofings, sky walks and building interiors.



Since its inception in 2006, CBS has executed over 300 projects at numerous Retail Hubs across Delhi & NCR, and many other cities of North, West and East of India. Current projects consist of sports facilities, health facilities, commercial retail outlets, housings, hospitalities, educational institutions and infrastructure.



Equipped with an in-house Architectural, Engineering and Execution team, CBS provides highly efficient and effective execution of projects. This is combined with outstanding functionality with high aesthetic appeal enabling fascinating architectural solutions.
